Are you a Dartmouthian looking for a social run to add into your weekly routine?
Join us for a 6KM run every Thursday at 6:30PM. We meet rain, shine, sleet, or snow at the Sullivan's Pond Gazebo, near the intersection of Crichton Avenue and Hawthorne Street, in the heart of the Darkside!
